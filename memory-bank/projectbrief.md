### Why Does This Project Exist?

**Problem Statement:**
Business founders/operators often get trapped in operational bottlenecks, spending disproportionate time on manual, repetitive processes instead of strategic growth. Without systematic documentation and automation, these time drains compound and prevent delegation.

**Solution Approach:**
This toolkit combines two powerful components:
1. **Memory-Bank**: AI-optimized documentation framework that captures business processes
2. **MBIE**: Semantic search engine that identifies automation opportunities in documented processes

Together, they enable rapid business intelligence discovery and transformation planning.
